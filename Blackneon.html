Cory Catfish


Cory Catfish, also known as Corydoras, are a popular choice for community tanks due to their peaceful nature and unique appearance. They are bottom-dwelling scavengers and are known for their ability to ¬clean the aquarium substrate. One important aspect to consider is that they prefer to live in groups of at least 5-6 individuals, so make sure you have enough space for them.


  • Compatibility:5/5
  • Tank Size:minimum 20 gallons (76 liters) for a group
  • Care Level:Easy
  • Temperament:Peaceful
  • Diet & Feeding:Omnivorous; sinking pellets, frozen/live foods, and occasional vegetables
  • Adult Size:1-3 inches (2.5-7.6 cm) depending on the species
