When purchasing tickets, you will have three accompanying options to choose from: Basic, Silver, and Gold.

Basic ticket

The Basic ticket includes the following services:

Enjoy on-land games.
Explore the aquarium.

Silver ticket

The Silver ticket includes all services in the Basic ticket, plus:
Participate in water diving activities.
Engage in light-hearted games.

Gold ticket

The Gold ticket includes all services in the Silver ticket, plus:
Enjoy a variety of self-service dishes.
Experience thrilling games.

NOTE:

Once you make a purchase online, the QR Code will be sent to your email immediately after a successful transaction confirmation.

Upon entering the automatic gate, all you need to do is scan the received QR Code. You can easily gift tickets to your friends or family by sending them the QR Code.

Please note that each QR Code can only be scanned once and will become invalid afterward.
Buying tickets online allows you to take control of your time and save waiting time when you arrive, especially during peak periods.

Welcome to International Children's Day on June 1st


We are thrilled to invite you to join us at Nexus Aquarium on the occasion of International Children's Day on June 1st. It is a day dedicated to celebrating and honoring the innocence, joy, and boundless potential of children worldwide.


At Nexus Aquarium, we believe in creating unforgettable experiences for children and fostering their love and appreciation for the magnificent marine world. On this special day, we have planned an array of exciting activities and engaging exhibits designed to spark curiosity and ignite imaginations.

From interactive touch tanks to captivating educational presentations, our team of marine experts will be on hand to guide children through a journey of discovery. They will have the opportunity to learn about various marine species, their habitats, and the importance of conservation.

In addition to the educational aspect, we have also prepared delightful surprises and entertaining performances to make this day even more memorable. Face painting, balloon art, and live music will add an extra touch of joy and amusement.


We understand the significance of this day, and our utmost priority is to ensure the safety and well-being of all our young visitors. We have implemented strict health and safety protocols, including limited capacity, social distancing measures, and enhanced sanitation procedures.

Join us at Nexus Aquarium as we celebrate the spirit and wonder of childhood on International Children's Day. Let us create unforgettable memories together and inspire the next generation of ocean enthusiasts.

Welcome to the Reunification Day of Vietnam on April 30th and International Labor Day on May 1st

Welcome to Nexus Aquarium's event page dedicated to celebrating the Reunification Day of Vietnam and International Labor Day on April 30th. On this special occasion, we honor the historical significance of the reunification of South Vietnam and the contributions of workers worldwide.


At Nexus Aquarium, we recognize the importance of both events and their impact on our nation and the global labor force. To commemorate these important milestones, we have curated a series of activities and exhibits that highlight the spirit of unity, resilience, and the value of labor.

Join us for a day filled with educational presentations, interactive workshops, and engaging displays that showcase the relationship between labor and the marine world. Learn about the essential role of workers in the aquarium industry, from animal care and exhibit maintenance to conservation efforts and environmental stewardship.

We also invite you to explore our special exhibitions that pay tribute to the contributions of workers in various fields, including marine research, sustainability, and community development. Discover inspiring stories of individuals who have dedicated their careers to creating positive change and preserving our precious natural resources. As we celebrate the Reunification Day and International Labor Day, we express our gratitude to all workers for their hard work, dedication, and commitment. We invite you to visit Nexus Aquarium on this day to not only enjoy the wonders of the marine world but also to recognize and appreciate the invaluable contributions of the labor force.

Come and be part of this special event where we celebrate the unity of our nation and honor the achievements of workers across industries. Let us collectively inspire one another and pave the way for a sustainable and prosperous future.

Plan your visit to Nexus Aquarium on April 30th to commemorate these significant occasions and experience a day of learning, appreciation, and celebration. We look forward to welcoming you and sharing this memorable journey together
